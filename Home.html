Welcome to CVR College of Engineering

Established in 2001, CVR College of Engineering (CVRCE) is a premier autonomous institution located in Ibrahimpatnam, Greater Hyderabad, Telangana. Promoted by the Cherabuddi Education Society, it is consistently ranked in the top 151–200 engineering colleges nationally by the NIRF.
